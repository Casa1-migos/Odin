Delicous Pie

This recipe will show you how to make a delicous pie all your family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • Pie crust
  • 4 apples

How to make a apple pie

  1. Pre heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Peel apples then slice into thin pieces
  3. Place pie crust into baking dish
  4. Mix apples in brown sugar
  5. Place apples into Pie crust
  6. Put top of pie crust
  7. Poke holes into pie crust as shown in image 3

    8. Image 3 pie with holes

  8. Bake pie for 30 minuets

  9. Enjoy your pie ;)
