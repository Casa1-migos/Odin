Tasty lasgna

This recpie will show you how to make high protien tasty lasgna

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cheese
  • Ground Turkey
  • Tomato Sauce
  • lasgna noodles

How to make lasgna

  1. Warm up oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cook ground turkey on stove over medium-high heat
  3. Boil lasgna noodles for 10 minuets
  4. Add boiled lasgna noodles to bottom of baking dish
  5. Add cooked ground turkey to top of noodles
  6. Add noodles ontop of ground turkey
  7. Add Tomato Sauce
  8. Add cheese to top of lasgna
  9. Bake for 5 minuets
  10. Finally enjoy high protien lasgna
